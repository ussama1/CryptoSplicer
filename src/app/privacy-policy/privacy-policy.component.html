1. Personal information

What Personal information is collected?

In a normal case CryptoSlicer "Site" is not collecting any personally identifiable information. However there are some cases where CryptoSlicer might need visitor's email address in order to perform a service (ex. Newsletter, Submit a story, Contact form etc.). In such cases email address will be explicitly requested and visitor notified. Once collected these emails are used for the sole purpose of the function intended by visitor. They will not be passed to any third party.

When is the information collected?

When a student registers on our site he is only asked to give his name and email. Afterwards for transfer of money he is to provide his credit card information. It is mandatory for the instructor to provide name, email and credit card information for money transactions.

Why this information is collected?

This information is collected to:

  • Personalize user’s experience
  • Improve customer service
  • Administer contests, surveys and promotions
  • Process transactions
  • Reviews
  • Follow ups
  • Periodic emails

9. Bidding

How it works?

This is our unique feature. Instructors put up courses for auction. Students are to place bids on the course of their interest. The one with the highest bid shall get the course.

Maximum/minimum number of bids

Unlimited bids can be made by one student. Unlimited courses can be put up by instructors.

Time limit

The instructor shall specify the ending time of bidding. Time limit of bidding on a course may vary from instructor to instructor.

Commitment

Once you’ve placed your bid and won, you cannot withdraw from the course.
